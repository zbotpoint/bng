Frequently Asked Questions

What is DUSTED?

DUSTED is short for Drink an Unsuspecting Small-Town Establishment Dry. It's where you and a whole bunch of your peers get on a bus, drive out to the middle of nowhere, and have a good time at a bar. As a bonus goal, we try to consume all the alcohol at the fine establishment.

Where are we going?

Shh - it's a secret.

Do I have to drink?

No! You're welcome to come along for the fun times and bars often serve non-alcoholic drinks. The ticket cost we charge is to cover the cost of the bus ride.

I can't drink that much; do I have to drink a lot?

The Blue & Gold Committee recommends that attendees drink responsibly and know their limit.

I'm under the legal drinking age in Ontario (19 years). Can I come?

No. Keep an eye out for future DUSTED and other Blue & Gold Committee events in the future!

Do I need to know how to drive?

We rent a bus for the night so that attendees can safely get from and to the venue. The ticket cost is to partially offset the cost of renting the bus for all attendees.
