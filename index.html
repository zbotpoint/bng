The Blue & Gold Committee


A SPECIAL KIND OF SOCIAL COMMITTEE

What do we do?

The Blue & Gold Committee has two jobs:

(i) To encourage and provide the resources for students to develop their practical engineering skills, and

(ii) to host unique events that strengthen the Skule™ community.


You may be surprised to find out just how much the Blue & Gold Committee is responsible for within the Skule community. Blue & Gold manages the tools in the Blue & Gold room, coordinates large community builds like the pride parade float, schedules work sessions for design-based courses, and organizes all sorts of fun community events like Godiva Week, the Frosh Week bed races, Tools 101 and 102, and more.

The most important thing for you to know is that Blue & Gold has your back.

Need to borrow tools for the evening? Let us know.
Have a cool new idea for an off-campus (or on-campus) event? Let us know.
Need help designing a table, light display, or killer robot? Let us know.

Just wanna chat about life and the universe? Let us know!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the "Blue and Gold Committee?"

The Blue and Gold Committee is a UofT Engineering social committee that organizes community events, manages tools and design resources available to students, and bridges the gap between what you learn in class and what you build in the real world.

Who are you?

We are Nathan and Sophia (both ECE 2T2s), the Chairs of the Blue and Gold Committee!

How can I borrow tools?

Head on over to the Tools Page, fill out the Google form, and we’ll reach out to you via email. You can also send us an email at blueandgold@skule.ca.

How can I get involved?

Our favourite question!! Easiest way to get involved is to show up to Blue & Gold events. And the easiest way to find out about Blue & Gold events is to connect with us on social media. Here are the links for easy access:
Facebook: Blue and Gold Committee
Instagram: @bngcommittee
Mailing List: Sign up here

Can my club borrow tools too?

For the most part, yes. We're happy to help out and loan tools to other clubs on a short-term basis. Process is the same as individuals borrowing, just make sure to indicate that you are a club on the form. If you need the tools for more than 24 hours, email us at blueandgold@skule.ca and we can discuss the logistics of it all.

What kinds of tools does the Blue & Gold Committee have?

An easier question would be what kind of tools doesn't the Blue & Gold Committee have. No really, we have so many different kinds of stuff. Check out the tools page to check out some of our more popular tools, but we hope to publish a more comprehensive inventory list for your convenience. If you’re unsure whether or not we have something, send us an email.

What events do you run?

We run a variety of different social and build events every year, but you can check out our annual events here. We’re always looking for new ideas, so if you have an idea for a new event, let us know!
