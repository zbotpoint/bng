The Blue & Gold Committee


A SPECIAL KIND OF SPIRIT COMMITTEE

What do we do?

The Blue & Gold Committee has two jobs:

(i) To encourage and provide the resources for students to develop their practical engineering skills, and

(ii) to host unique events that strengthen the Skule™ community.


You may be surprised to find out just how much the Blue & Gold Committee is responsible for within the Skule™ Community. Blue & Gold manages the tools in the Blue & Gold room, coordinates large community builds like the pride parade float, schedules work sessions for design-based courses, and organizes all sorts of fun community events like Godiva Week, the F!rosh Week Bed Races, Tools 101 & 102, and much more. You may even see us handing out hot chocolate in the pit, running blood drives, and trips off campus.

The most important thing for you to know is that Blue & Gold has your back.

Need to borrow tools for the evening? Let us know.
Have a cool new idea for an off-campus (or on-campus) event? Let us know.
Need help designing a table, light display, or killer robot? Let us know.

Just wanna chat about life and the universe? Let us know!
