Frequently Asked Questions

Can I come to events? Do I have to register?

Everyone is welcome! No need to register, just show up and enjoy. We'll have everything ready for you beforehand. If there's anything you should bring or do before you come, we'll let you know on our social media and emails!

What is a "build" and how is it any different than a prank?

A build is creation that a bunch of engineers get together to, well, build. Often they're made to celebrate a recent holiday or event. Pranks, on the other hand, critically involve an element of How did they do that??  Not all pranks are builds, and not all builds are pranks.

Remembrance Day

As engineers, we hold our duty to society paramount. On Remembrance Day, we honour the those that fought and died to protect our way of life. Every year, the Skule™ Community builds a monument to honour the University of Toronto Engineering students that fought and died in the two world wars. Engineers Remember the Wars.

Date: The day before November 11th
